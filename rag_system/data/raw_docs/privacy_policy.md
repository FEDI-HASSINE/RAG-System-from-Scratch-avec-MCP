# Privacy Policy

## Introduction

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected and that all data handling complies with applicable regulations.

## User Data Collection

We collect the following types of personal information:

- **Account Information**: Name, email address, phone number
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with our services, pages visited, features used
- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, IP address
- **Payment Information**: Billing address, payment method details (encrypted)

### How We Collect Data

Data is collected through:

1. Direct input when you create an account or fill out forms
2. Automatic collection through cookies and similar technologies
3. Third-party services that you connect to your account

## Data Protection Measures

We implement robust security measures to protect your data:

- All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3
- Personal information is stored in encrypted databases
- Access to user data is restricted to authorized personnel only
- Regular security audits are conducted by independent firms
- Multi-factor authentication is required for all administrative access

## GDPR Compliance

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

### Right to Access
You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you. We will provide this within 30 days of your request.

### Right to Rectification
If any information we hold about you is incorrect, you can request that we correct it. Simply contact our support team with the correct information.

### Right to Erasure
Also known as the "right to be forgotten", you can request that we delete all your personal data. Note that some information may be retained for legal compliance purposes.

### Right to Data Portability
You can request your data in a machine-readable format that can be transferred to another service provider.

## Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary:

- Active account data: Retained while your account is active
- Transaction records: Retained for 7 years for legal compliance
- Usage analytics: Anonymized after 24 months
- Marketing preferences: Retained until you opt out
